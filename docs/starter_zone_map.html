River of Dawn - Starter Zone: Dead Sea Shore

Map Legend

# Cliff / Solid Rock (impassable)
X Brine Pits (impassable, toxic fumes)
~ Dead Sea (impassable water)
. Walkable Ground (sand, dirt, rock)
: Worn Footpath (keeper's trail)
P Player Spawn Point
G Cave Entrance (locked, needs key)
S Shrine of Ba'al (ruined)
D Hyena Encounter (combat)
K Torn Satchel (empty)
L Loot (dagger + armor)
! Skeleton Keeper (note)
B Lore Stone (Dead Sea marker)

Player Flow

  1. Spawn at P on the Dead Sea shore
  2. Move north → find L (loot: dagger + armor)
  3. Continue north → find ! (skeleton + note about satchel)
  4. Move north → read B (lore stone identifying Dead Sea)
  5. Brine pits block east → move west into canyon
  6. Find K (torn satchel - empty, no key)
  7. Exit canyon → D (hyena) attacks!
  8. Defeat hyena → key drops from belly
  9. Optional: visit S (Shrine of Ba'al)
  10. Follow path to G (cave entrance)
  11. Use key → enter cave → transition to PassageToYeriho
